Voice Leads

Once your Voice Lead Channel is active you can immediately begin generating revenue by delivering live consumers directly to buyers.

To maintain an Active voice lead channel, it is required that a minimum amount of voice leads are traded in any 30-day period. Each varied source of traffic (website, radio) should have a unique 800 number.

Be sure to review marketplace guidelines in Trading Rules along with marketing and conversion tips in Best Practices.
