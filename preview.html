* TRACKING KEYS: The Evolute systems allows for the seller submission of four unique identifiers: SID, CID, MID and TID.
Our recommendation is to use at least a unique identifier for each lead (SID) and a unique identifier for each campaign (CID);
For example, SID=123XK5 and CID=142GS8. SID, CID, MID and TID are the default tracking keys,
but you can use the Evolute system to enter a customized tracking key, for example you can change SID to SESSION_ID.
** SECURE RIGHTS: Evolute security measures require you to deliver a token with each lead. The token is
created by Evolute and inserted into a hidden field in the form. This field should be at the top of your lead form,
have type "hidden" and name "SR_TOKENquot;. See documentation on Security Token.
